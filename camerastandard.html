Camera C500 Standard

Capture Every Moment in Stunning Detail

Overview

The Camera C500 Standard offers a perfect balance of performance and portability, designed for photography enthusiasts who want to elevate their creativity. With its high-resolution sensor and fast autofocus system, the C500 delivers crisp and vibrant photos whether you’re shooting landscapes, portraits, or action scenes.

Key Features

  • 24.2MP APS-C CMOS Sensor for exceptional image quality
  • Fast Hybrid Autofocus with 425 focus points
  • 4K video recording at 30fps
  • 3.0" tilting touchscreen LCD for easy framing
  • Built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity
  • Compact and lightweight body for easy travel
