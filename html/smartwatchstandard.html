Smartwatch S3 Standard

Smart Fitness, Simplified

Overview

The Smartwatch S3 Standard is designed for users who want a balanced mix of style, performance, and daily functionality. Whether tracking your fitness goals, monitoring your heart rate, or staying connected on the go, the S3 Standard keeps everything simple, smart, and efficient.

Key Features

  • 24/7 heart rate and sleep monitoring
  • Step counter and multiple workout modes
  • Water-resistant design (IP68 rating)
  • 5-day battery life on a single charge
  • Bluetooth notifications for calls, texts, and apps
