Overview

The Camera C500 Pro takes professional photography to the next level. Designed for creators, vloggers, and studio photographers, it features an advanced full-frame sensor, ultra-fast autofocus, and high dynamic range imaging for unmatched detail and color accuracy.

Key Features

  • 42.4 MP Full-Frame BSI CMOS Sensor
  • NextGen Image Processor X2
  • Real-time Eye Autofocus and Object Tracking
  • 8K Video Recording at 30 fps
  • Dual SD Card Slots for Backup Storage
  • 5-Axis In-Body Image Stabilization (IBIS)
  • Rugged Weather-Sealed Body
