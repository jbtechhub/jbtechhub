Overview

The Smartphone Z10 Standard delivers everything you need in a modern phone — fast performance, long battery life, and a stunning display — all in a sleek design. Perfect for students, professionals, and casual users, the Z10 Standard offers exceptional value with flagship-level features at a friendly price.

  • 6.7" FHD+ AMOLED 120Hz display
  • Triple rear cameras (108MP + 12MP + 8MP)

  • 5000mAh battery with 67W fast charging
  • Side-mounted fingerprint + face unlock
