Smartwatch S3 Pro

Smart Power, Stylish Innovation

Overview

The Smartwatch S3 Pro is built for those who want both functionality and fashion. With its sleek design, vibrant AMOLED display, and long-lasting battery, it’s the perfect fitness and productivity companion for your daily routine.

Key Features

  • 1.8” AMOLED Always-On display with 60Hz refresh rate
  • Heart rate, SpO₂, sleep, and stress monitoring
  • Bluetooth calling and AI voice assistant
  • IP68 water resistance and fitness tracking for 100+ sports modes
  • Battery life up to 10 days on normal use
