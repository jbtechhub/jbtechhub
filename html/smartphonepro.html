Smartphone Z10 Pro

Professional Power. Limitless Creativity.

Overview

The Smartphone Z10 Pro is built for creators, gamers, and professionals who demand top-tier performance without compromise. Featuring a Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 processor, pro-grade cameras, and a 2K dynamic AMOLED display, the Z10 Pro delivers an immersive experience in every touch, swipe, and shot. Designed with precision and elegance, it’s a device that works as hard as you do.

Key Features

  • Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 (4nm) for ultra-fast and efficient performance
  • 18GB LPDDR5X RAM + 1TB UFS 4.0 Storage
  • 6.9" Dynamic AMOLED 2K Display with 165Hz refresh rate
  • Quad Camera System (200MP + 48MP + 12MP + 8MP)
  • 40MP front camera with AI-enhanced portraits
  • 6000mAh battery with 120W HyperCharge
  • IP69 dust/water resistant body with Gorilla Glass Victus 2
  • Dolby Atmos Stereo Speakers + Hi-Res Audio
  • 5G, Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.5, NFC, GPS
