Tablet A7 Standard

Smart. Portable. Reliable Everyday Tablet.

Overview

The Tablet A7 Standard is built for users who want reliable performance for studying, online meetings, and entertainment. With a large display, long-lasting battery, and optimized Android system, this tablet offers the perfect balance between productivity and portability — ideal for students, professionals, and families.

Key Features

  • 10.1″ HD IPS Display for clear and vibrant visuals
  • Octa-Core processor for smooth multitasking
  • 4GB RAM + 64GB internal storage (expandable up to 256GB)
  • 6000mAh battery for all-day use
  • 8MP rear + 5MP front camera for calls and photography
  • Dual SIM with 4G LTE connectivity and Wi-Fi support
  • Android 13 OS, optimized for performance and battery life
