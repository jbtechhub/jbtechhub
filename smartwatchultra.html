Smartwatch S3 Ultra

Overview

The Smartwatch S3 Ultra takes innovation and toughness to the next level. Designed for extreme conditions, this device blends a reinforced titanium frame, AMOLED display, and next-generation health tracking. Whether you’re hiking, swimming, or training, it’s built to go the distance — and beyond.

  • Up to 10 days battery life, fast wireless charging
  • Supports Bluetooth calls and notifications
  • Over 120 sports modes for all activities
