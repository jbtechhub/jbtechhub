Tablet A7 Pro

Professional Power in a Slim Body

Overview

The Tablet A7 Pro combines professional performance with sleek portability. Designed for creatives, multitaskers, and professionals who demand power and precision, this tablet is equipped with a high-performance processor, stunning 2K display, and advanced stylus support—perfect for productivity and creativity on the go.

Key Features

  • Octa-core processor with enhanced AI performance
  • 12GB RAM + 256GB storage (expandable up to 1TB)
  • 2K Ultra Vision display (2560×1600) with 120Hz refresh rate
  • Supports Smart Stylus Pro for note-taking and drawing
  • 10,000mAh battery with 45W fast charging
  • Quad speakers tuned by Dolby Atmos
  • Aluminum unibody design for premium durability
