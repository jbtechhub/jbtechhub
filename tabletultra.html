Tablet A7 Ultra

Performance Meets Elegance.

Overview

The Tablet A7 Ultra redefines what a mid-range tablet can do — combining premium design, fast performance, and immersive visuals. Powered by a high-speed octa-core processor and a stunning 2K display, it’s built for users who multitask, create, and stream without compromise.

Key Features

  • 12.4″ 2K IPS Display with ultra-slim bezels
  • Octa-Core 2.4GHz Processor for powerful multitasking
  • 8GB RAM and 256GB Storage (expandable up to 1TB)
  • Quad Speaker System with Dolby Atmos
  • 13MP rear + 8MP front camera with AI enhancements
  • Massive 8000mAh battery with 33W fast charging
  • 5G + Wi-Fi 6 Connectivity
  • Android 14 OS with smooth and responsive UI
